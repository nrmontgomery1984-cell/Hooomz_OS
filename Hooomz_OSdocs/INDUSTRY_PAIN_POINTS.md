# Hooomz OS - Industry Pain Points Research

This document captures the validated industry problems that Hooomz OS is designed to solve. All data comes from industry surveys, reports, and studies.

---

## The Core Problem

Small residential contractors (1-10 person companies) are underserved by existing construction software. Enterprise tools are too complex and expensive. Consumer tools lack the depth needed for professional work. The result: most small contractors cobble together spreadsheets, texts, paper, and maybe QuickBooks.

**Target User Profile:**
- 1-10 person company
- Owner is estimator, PM, might swing hammer
- No/minimal dedicated office staff
- Can't afford hours on data entry
- Running 1-5 active projects
- Cash flow is personal
- Currently using spreadsheets, texts, paper

### Communication & Rework Costs

**PlanGrid/FMI Research:**
- **$177 billion** - Annual labor costs from communication/rework
- **$31 billion** - Annual cost from miscommunication and poor data
- **2 full days per week** - Time workers lose solving avoidable issues
- **48%** - Rework attributed to poor communication/data

**PMI Data:**
- **1/3 of projects fail** primarily due to poor communication
- Projects with minimal communication:
  - Only 37% finish on time
  - Only 48% stay within budget
  - Only 52% meet their goals

### Project Failure Rates

- **70%** - Overall project failure rate
- Poor project management is the main reason
- **16% minimum** - Average budget overrun

## Subcontractor Pain Points

### Billd 2025 Report

**Cash Flow Crisis:**
| Issue | Stat |
|-------|------|
| Retain half+ profits to fund operations | 40% |
| Face payment waits over 30 days | 82% (up from 49% two years ago) |
| Lack working capital for unexpected expenses | 43% |
| Annual cost of slow payments | $280 billion |

**Payment Reality Gap:**
- GCs think payment occurs in: **30 days**
- Subs actually wait: **56 days** average

**Behavioral Changes:**
- 56% walked away from projects due to cash flow risk
- 42% pay their subs before getting paid themselves
- 100% now consider GC payment reputation when bidding
- 75% raise bids to account for payment delays

### Loops Advantage

The Loops architecture specifically addresses:

1. **Communication**: Everything happens in context. Notes, photos, time, checklists all attached to the right loop/task.

2. **Flexibility**: Small contractors don't work in rigid hierarchies. Loops nest however makes sense for the project.

3. **Health Visibility**: Status bubbles up automatically. Problems surface before they become crises.

4. **Documentation**: Activity Log captures everything. Disputes become "here's what happened" not "he said/she said."

5. **Client Trust**: Client Portal gives homeowners visibility without overwhelming them.

## Competitive Gap

**Enterprise Tools (Procore, Buildertrend):**
- Too expensive for 1-10 person companies
- Too complex; requires training
- Designed for large teams with dedicated office staff

**Consumer Tools (spreadsheets, texts):**
- No integration; data lives everywhere
- No accountability; things fall through cracks
- No client visibility

**Hooomz OS Positioning:**
- Right-sized for small contractors
- Loops match how they already think
- Mobile-first for field use
- Client Portal builds trust
- Activity Log protects everyone
